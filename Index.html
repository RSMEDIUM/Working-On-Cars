About Working on Cars

What It Involves

Prior to my interest in technology I was a mechanic for five years. Four being coporate with Hyundai, and one being a independant shop. Over the years I have learned that working on cars is a perfect blend of creativity and technical skill.

Many people start working on cars to save money on repairs and develop valuable technical skills.

My Car Projects

Engine Maintenance

Regular maintenance like oil changes, spark plug replacement, and fluid checks helps keep vehicles running properly. Several enging rebuilds and electric conversions as well.

Performance Upgrades

Installing performance parts such as air intakes, exhaust systems, and suspension upgrades improves vehicle performance.

Why I Enjoy Working on Cars

Working on cars builds problem-solving skills and mechanical knowledge. It is satisfying to repair or improve a vehicle yourself.

This hobby also helps save money and provides valuable real-world experience.
